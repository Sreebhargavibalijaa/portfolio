Sree Bhargavi Balija

University of Missouri, Columbia · MS UC San Diego · BE IIT Hyderabad

I build and study federated learning systems, privacy-preserving ML, and agentic AI infrastructure — spanning LLM efficiency & quantization, differential privacy, decentralized multi-agent frameworks, neuromorphic computing, and trustworthy AI. My research asks how intelligent systems can collaborate without centralizing data, and how LLMs can be made efficient and trustworthy at scale. I publish at AAAI, NeurIPS, and collaborate with MIT Media Lab, Meta Research, and Stanford.
